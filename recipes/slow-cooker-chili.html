Slow Cooker Chili

This slow cooker chili is packed with beans, veggies, and spices, and simply simmers away in your crockpot to make a delicious beefy chili.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions

Steps

  1. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  2. In the slow cooker, combine the browned beef, kidney beans, black beans, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, beef broth, chopped onion, bell pepper, minced garlic, chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stir everything together until well combined.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  5. Before serving, taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite toppings like shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped green onions.
